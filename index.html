Cover Letter 2

Dear Sir or Madam,

I found the job listing for a creative coder by a lucky coincidence when considering opportunities as a game developer in London. Nevertheless, my conviction that this would be the right type of position for me had only strengthened since. I believe that my experience with different aspects of computer graphics programming will help you tackle various technical challenges concerning visualization and animation, and my passion towards visual design will help us create even more daring and original solutions.

3 years ago, with the beginning of my studies at the Technical University of Munich, I changed my specialization from the web development to the graphics programming in order to incorporate my personal interest towards CG into my professional life. Up until now, I managed to try myself in low- and high-level CG programming, and finished several Mixed Reality projects. The most interesting for me personally, were the projects, where I got to follow my artistic vision and exercise my skill with different graphics and modeling suites. One of such projects was a Virtual Reality interpretation of a drawing by Edward Hopper, Nighthawks in 3D.

Having always considered game development to be the main option, which would allow me to combine my programming skills and the passion for all the things CG, I was delighted to find an opening for a developer at a creative studio. If I had to imagine myself in 5 to 10 years, I would be involved in or curating projects along the lines of digital storytelling, be it games or interactive experiences. I believe that there could be no better place for me to hone my skills than at a creative studio, because you do exactly that—you tell a story, whether visual, or aural—an emotional story of a brand, you communicate the idea by awaking feelings in the viewer. And that is, what excites me.

To conclude, procedural animation might be my strongest point yet, but the up-to-date experience I have with programming CG should allow for an easy adoption of the skill. I believe that our collaboration would be a mutually beneficial one: you get a developer who is passionate about CG and has an outlook that goes beyond the edges of a code editor window, and I get to apply my skills and develop in the desired direction, learning from new experience.

Kind regards,

Vadym Strelchenko.

Portfolio 3

Workspace awareness in Virtual Reality: Digital collaboration in urban planning phases, Master’s Thesis (https://tinyurl.com/ThesisGitRepo), 7 month (30 ECTS)


Tags: Unity3d, Git, Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, Blender, Adobe Photoshop

In this study I explored how aware people are of one another in VR environments when performing their individual architectural design-related tasks. During this 7-month long project I got to really deepen my knowledge of Unity3d and VR development in the engine. After 5 user studies and about thirty participants, I concluded that audio feedback is an invaluable tool that considerably enhances workspace awareness in such environments, both in combination with visual cues and by itself.

Tags: Android Studio, MetaIO SDK, AR, Git, Blender, Adobe Photoshop
